# Cost Notes

## Cost Posture

- PaperBinder optimizes for low recurring operational cost.
- Avoid platform features that require ongoing paid infrastructure unless they are directly necessary for core demo goals.
- Prefer single-database, in-process execution patterns.

## Dependency and Service Decisions

- Prefer built-in platform capabilities before adding external services.
- Hosted/managed dependencies require explicit justification tied to measurable risk reduction or necessary functionality.
